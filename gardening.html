How to Create a Fruit & Vegetable Garden

Fresh fruit and vegetables are an essential part of our everyday diets, providing vitamins that keep us healthy and strong. Here we intend to create a fruit and vegetable garden.

If you’ve eaten a tomato fresh from a bush, or a strawberry straight from the plant. You’ll already know that homegrown fruit and vegetables taste much better, and they are also packed with more nutrients. You should be able to grow your own fruit and vegetables, regardless of space, time, or skill.

how to create a basic fruit and vegetable garden which will slot in together with your wants and wishes . Identify a site for your fruit and vegetable garden. This could be a single border, a few pots on your patio or balcony, or a full-sized allotment. As long as it’s in a sunny spot in the garden.

If you’re planting directly into the soil, it’s a good idea to dig the soil over. Maybe add some compost, to introduce more nutrients into the soil. If you’re planting into containers, grab a few bags of compost.
