About The Food Garden

Introduction to the food garden

The food garden came about after a bout of ill health. I needed to make some lifestyle changes and a healthier diet needed to become a part of my daily routine. I started making some healthier meals. Watching my calorie intake, keeping my body in the best of health. And transforming my garden for growing fruit and vegetables to use for cooking in my new healthier diet.

Doing something about it and improve my eating habits. I needed to keep a record of my progress. Using this website was a way to record my progress. You can’t beat the flavors of freshly grown fruit and vegetables. And if growing them helped in getting my body healthy all the better.

Growing fruit and vegetables in the food garden

If like me you’ve no idea how to create an allotment to grow your own fruit and vegetables. I needed to start somewhere and try and find some advice. What to do on a limited budget and a limited labor force of just yourself to tackle the work.

If you’re looking to grow your own fruit and vegetables click on this link for the gardening section

Growing your own produce is the only way to enjoy delicious, garden-fresh fruit and vegetables all year round. Finding the proper tools and selecting, which plants to grow, to nurturing your crops and bringing in your first harvest.

My first job was to start clearing my overgrown garden of blackberry bushes which had overtaken my garden. I needed to get a hedge trimmer and a garden saw to help with the hard work.
