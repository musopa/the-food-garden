About The Food Garden

The food garden came about after a bout of ill health, some life style changes had to be made and a healthier diet needed to become a part of my daily routine. I decided to start growing my own fruit, vegetables, herbs, and spices in my garden and include them in my new healthier eating options creating some new recipes to go with my diet, so I needed to learn how to grow my own fruit, vegetables, herbs, and spices and to look at the best methods of cooking them to create some homemade gourmet recipes to provide some healthy eating options watch my calorie intake, and keeping my body in the best of health.
